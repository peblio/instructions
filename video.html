Videos

Add videos to from Youtube, Vimeo, or Google Drive.

Google Drive Vimeo Youtube

Google Drive

1. Upload your video to Google Drive.

2. Make sure that your video can be viewed by others. Right click and select "Get Shareable Link" to make sure that link sharing is on.

3. Double click on your video and select "Open in New Window" from the menu

4. Select "Embed item" from the menu and copy the text from the text area

5. Paste the text into the input below and click "Submit"

Vimeo

1. Log in to Vimeo and upload your video.

2. When your video is uploaded, hover over the video and hit the "Share" button.

3. Copy the text from the text area labled "Embed"

4. Paste the text into the input below and click "submit"

Youtube

1. Upload your video to youtube.

2. Click the "share" button under the video.

3. Select the "Embed" option.

4. Copy the text from the text area.

5. Paste the text into the input below and click "Submit".
