Peblio Embed Tool

You can add your slide deck, website, code from another web editor, or any other embedable media to your Pebl

We'll walk you through it!

Google Slides

1. Click on the “Publish to the web” option in the File menu.

2. A modal will pop up. Follow the instructions and click “Publish.”

3. Select the “Embed” tab and copy the content from the textbox

4. Paste the text into the input below and click “Submit”

Code Editors

There are many different editors on the web and many of them allow you to embed your code in other websites. We’ll go through a few common ones…

Codepen

1. Click “embed” which you’ll find in the bottom right corner of your pen.

2. Choose the “iframe” tab. Then copy and paste the text into the input below and click “Submit”

Repl.it

1. Click “embed” which you’ll find in the bottom right corner of your pen.

2. Choose the “iframe” tab. Then copy and paste the text into the input below and click “Submit”

3. Select the “Embed” tab and copy the content from the textbox
